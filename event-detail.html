About Event

“Global platform for the beauty industry”
International Beauty & Spa Expo is the most acclaimed and largest international beauty and spa event worldwide. This event shows product from Cosmetics and Beauty Products industry. The format of the event has been designed to offer an opportunity for best networking and business opportunities and provide an interactive platform for the traders, buyers & professionals. It is only business to business Indian beauty Trade show event and the only meeting place for the Global beauty industry to Network, Interact, Source Distributors, Partner, Witness Trends & Capsulate Business from the Indian Beauty Market.
